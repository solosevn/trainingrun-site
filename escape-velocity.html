Escape Velocity Methodology

Escape Velocity identifies the AI model with the most momentum - the one breaking free from the pack. This page documents our complete methodology with full source citations.

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Executive Summary

Escape Velocity measures which AI model is gaining ground fastest, combining performance improvement with competitive positioning and market impact.

Velocity Score = (TRS % Change × 0.5) + (Rank Positions Climbed × 0.3) + (Narrative Impact × 0.2)

Where TRS = Training Run Score, measuring overall model capability

Component Breakdown

1. TRS Percentage Change (50% Weight)

What We Measure

The week-over-week change in a model's Training Run Score. A model that improves from TRS 72 to TRS 78 gains 8.3% - significant momentum.
