Executive Summary

Escape Velocity measures which AI model is gaining ground fastest, combining performance improvement with competitive positioning and market impact.

Velocity Score = (TRS % Change × 0.5) + (Rank Positions Climbed × 0.3) + (Narrative Impact × 0.2)
div>

Where TRS = Training Run Score, measuring overall model capability

div>
section>

Component Breakdown

1. TRS Percentage Change (50% Weight)

What We Measure

The week-over-week change in a model's Training Run Score. A model that improves from TRS 72 to TRS 78 gains 8.3% - significant momentum.

div>

Data Source

Internal TRS calculations derived from LMSYS Arena, SWE-Bench, and ARC-AGI-2 benchmarks.

→ See TRS Methodologya>
div>
div>

2. Rank Positions Climbed (30% Weight)

What We Measure

How many positions the model moved up in our overall rankings. Moving from #8 to #5 represents three positions climbed - strong competitive momentum.

div>

Calculation

Previous week rank minus current week rank. Positive values indicate climbing. Normalized to 0-100 scale where 5+ positions = maximum score.

div>
div>

3. Narrative Impact (20% Weight)

What We Measure

Market significance of the model's improvement - new capabilities announced, benchmark records broken, or significant competitive implications.

div>

Data Sources

Company announcements, benchmark leaderboards, verified news from Reuters/AP.
