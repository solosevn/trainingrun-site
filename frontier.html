Frontier Methodology

Frontier tracks humanity's progress toward AGI using the ARC-AGI-2 benchmark - the gold standard for measuring genuine reasoning ability. This page documents our complete methodology.

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

What is ARC-AGI-2?

The Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC) tests novel reasoning on tasks specifically designed to require thinking through new problems. Unlike traditional benchmarks, ARC tasks cannot be solved through memorization.

Why ARC-AGI-2?

Most AI benchmarks can be "gamed" through training data contamination. ARC-AGI-2 solves this by requiring genuine abstraction and reasoning - the core capabilities that would define AGI.
