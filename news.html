What is the Weekly News Download?

The Weekly News Download is Training Run's news segment, delivered by avatar Kennedy. Every headline passes through our rigorous 3-tier Truth Filter before making it to air.

Segment Summary

  • Avatar: Kennedy
  • Focus: Verified AI headlines from the past week
  • Headlines per Episode: Top 3 stories
  • Philosophy: Facts only, zero editorial opinion

The Truth Filter

Every news story must pass through our 3-tier verification system before inclusion.

3-Tier Truth Filter

Tier 1: Source Verification Is it from an approved source?
Tier 2: Fact Confirmation Can it be independently verified?
Tier 3: Opinion Removal Is it fact, not opinion?
