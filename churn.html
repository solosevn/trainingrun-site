Why "The Churn"?

"Labor churn" is the economic term for the rate of job creation and destruction flows. It captures the dual reality that jobs are being lost AND created simultaneously by AI.

What We Track

Jobs Displaced (Red)

Definition

Layoffs where AI/automation is cited as a factor in the company's announcement or press release.

Categories: Customer Service, Content Writing, Data Entry, Translation, Basic Analysis

challengergray.com/blog/category/job-cuts-report/ →a>

Jobs Created (Green)

Definition

New job postings in AI-related roles that didn't exist or were rare before the AI boom.

Categories: AI/ML Engineering, Prompt Engineering, AI Safety, AI Product Manager, AI Trainer

economicgraph.linkedin.com →a>
