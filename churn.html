The Churn Methodology

The Churn tracks the weekly flow of jobs being displaced and created by AI - the labor market in motion.
"Jobs leaving. Jobs arriving."

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Why "The Churn"?

"Labor churn" is the economic term for the rate of job creation and destruction flows. It captures the dual reality that jobs are being lost AND created simultaneously by AI.
