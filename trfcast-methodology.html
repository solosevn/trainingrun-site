TRFcast Methodology

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

TRFcast is an independent data axis measuring how well AI models predict the future — across forecasting, trading, calibration, financial reasoning, and market intelligence. Unlike TRS (which measures overall capability), TRFcast measures whether a model can see what's coming. This page documents our complete methodology with full source citations.

Why TRFcast Exists

AI models are increasingly used to make financial decisions, predict market movements, and forecast real-world events. But which models actually predict the future accurately? Existing benchmarks test knowledge and reasoning — not forecasting ability. TRFcast fills this gap with 5 independent pillars drawn from 4 live forecasting and trading platforms. Every score is derived from real predictions with verifiable outcomes. No synthetic benchmarks. No self-reported metrics. Just real forecasting performance.

TRFcast aggregates forecasting performance across five distinct dimensions, each measured on live platforms with real-world outcomes:

Pillar Details

Pillar 1: Forecasting Accuracy — 30%

ForecastBench Baseline Brier Score (20%)
Lower Brier = better calibrated predictions on real-world questions
ForecastBench Tournament Performance (10%)
Competitive ranking against other models in structured prediction tournaments
Source: ForecastBench (forecastbench.org)

Pillar 2: Trading Performance — 25%

Rallies.ai Returns (15%)
Actual portfolio performance on the Rallies.ai platform
Alpha Arena Returns (10%)
Returns from nof1.ai's Alpha Arena competitive trading environment
Sources: Rallies.ai + nof1.ai Alpha Arena

Pillar 3: Prediction Calibration — 20%

ForecastBench Calibration (20%)
How well a model's confidence levels match actual outcome frequencies
Source: ForecastBench (forecastbench.org)

Pillar 4: Financial Reasoning — 15%

FinanceArena QA Accuracy (8%)
Percentage correct on financial question-answering benchmarks
FinanceArena Comparative ELO (7%)
Head-to-head reasoning comparison rankings
Source: FinanceArena (financearena.ai)

Pillar 5: Market Intelligence — 10%

Alpha Arena Sharpe Ratio (5%)
Risk-adjusted return quality from nof1.ai
Rallies.ai Win Rate (5%)
Consistency of winning trades on the Rallies.ai platform
Sources: Alpha Arena Sharpe + Rallies.ai Win Rate

Complete Formula

TRFcast combines all 9 sub-metrics into a single normalized score:

TRFcast = ForecastBench_Baseline (20%) + ForecastBench_Tournament (10%) + Rallies_Returns (15%) + AlphaArena_Returns (10%) + ForecastBench_Calibration (20%) + FinanceArena_QA (8%) + FinanceArena_Compare (7%) + AlphaArena_Sharpe (5%) + Rallies_WinRate (5%)

Models with null sub-metrics are scored only on available data, normalized proportionally. All source data is publicly verifiable.

Key Differentiator: Discovery-First Approach

TRFcast uses a discovery-first approach. Unlike benchmarks with fixed model lists, every daily update scans ALL models on ALL 4 source platforms. Any model from any company in the world can qualify if it appears across enough sources to produce scores in at least 3 of the 5 pillars. New entrants are discovered and added automatically.
