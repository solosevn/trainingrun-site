Deep Thought Methodology

Deep Thought measures how well AI models predict the future - testing their ability to forecast real-world events with accuracy and financial consequences.

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

The Formula

Deep Thought Score combines real-money trading performance with forecast accuracy benchmarks.

Deep Thought Score = (Normalized Trading Return x 0.5) + (Inverted Brier Score x 0.5)

Higher scores = better at predicting the future

Component 1: Trading Performance (50%)

AI Prediction Arena

What It Measures

Real-money trading returns from AI models making predictions on real events. Models put skin in the game by trading on prediction markets.

predictionarena.ai →

Normalization

Returns are normalized to 0-100 scale relative to the best-performing model in the current period.

Component 2: Forecast Accuracy (50%)

ForecastBench

What It Measures

Prediction accuracy on real-world forecasting questions. Uses Brier scores where lower scores indicate better calibrated predictions.

forecastbench.org →

Normalization

Brier scores are inverted (1 - Brier) and normalized to 0-100 scale relative to the best-performing model in the current period.
