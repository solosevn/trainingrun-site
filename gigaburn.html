Data Categories

1. Deployed Capacity (Green)

What It Measures

Gigawatts (GW) of active capacity companies currently have operational.

Source: Annual Sustainability Reports, Environmental Reports

div>
div>

2. Announced Capacity (Yellow)

What It Measures

Gigawatts (GW) companies have committed to build, with target year.

Source: SEC EDGAR 10-K/10-Q Filings, Press Releases, Earnings Calls

div>
div>

3. Online This Year (Cyan)

What It Measures

Megawatts (MW) of new facilities coming online in current year.

Source: Company Newsrooms, Data Center Dynamics

Conversion Reference

  • 1 GW = 1,000 MW
  • TWh/year / 8,760 hours = Average GW
  • Example: 25 TWh/year = ~2.9 GW average
