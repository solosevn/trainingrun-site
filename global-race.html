How to Verify Each Component

1. Research Output (arXiv)

Visit arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recenta>, count papers by author affiliation country, cross-reference with Semantic Scholar for citations.

div>
div>

2. Model Releases (Epoch AI)

Visit epoch.ai/mlinputs/notable-modelsa>, count new models by country of origin.

div>
div>

3. Funding Activity (Crunchbase)

Search AI startups by country, filter by funding date, sum total funding by country.

div>
div>

4. Compute Power (TOP500)

Visit top500.orga>, sum total exaflops by country.

div>
div>

5. Benchmark Performance (LMSYS)

Visit chat.lmsys.org leaderboarda>, identify country of origin for top 20 models, calculate average Elo by country.

div>
div>

6. Policy Moves (OECD.AI)

Visit oecd.ai/en/dashboardsa>, check for new AI regulations and government investment announcements.
