How TRScode is Calculated

TRScode is a weighted composite of 8 sub-metrics drawn from 7 independent platforms. Each model is scored 0–100 on each sub-metric (top performer = 100, others proportional). The weighted sum produces the final daily TRScode score.

TRScode = SWE-bench_Verified (17%) + SWE-rebench (13%) + LiveCodeBench (15%) + BigCodeBench (10%) + Terminal-Bench_Hard (12%) + SWE-bench_Pro (8%) + SciCode (15%) + Arena_Code_ELO (10%)

Qualification: Models must have non-null scores in at least 2 of 5 pillars. Null handling: Missing pillars contribute zero (Option B) — ensuring frontier models with broad coverage always outrank niche performers.

All source data is publicly verifiable. Discovery-first methodology ensures every model with sufficient benchmark coverage is included automatically.
